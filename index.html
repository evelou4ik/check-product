Introduction

Step into the enchanting world of Harry Potter and embark on an exciting quest through the streets of London. The London Outdoor Escape Game: Harry Potter Movie Locations is a unique experience that combines the magic of the beloved Harry Potter series with the thrill of a city exploration. With just your smartphone as your guide, you will follow clues and solve puzzles to discover iconic filming locations from the movies. As you unlock new stories and secrets, you will visit the National Gallery, Trafalgar Square, Cecil Court, Covent Garden, and more. Immerse yourself in the wizarding world as Theodgar, a powerful Wizard who has chosen to protect the human race. Prepare to face Ozin, a dangerous sorcerer who aims to destroy Earth. This immersive adventure is a must-do for all Harry Potter fans and anyone seeking a thrilling and interactive exploration of London's hidden treasures.

What to expect?

With the London Outdoor Escape Game, you can expect an exhilarating quest filled with clues, puzzles, and intriguing stories. Upon arriving at the starting point, simply open the app on your smartphone to receive your first clue. Each clue will guide you from one magical location to another, providing you with precise directions along the way. No need for maps, GPS, or a guide – everything you need is right there on your phone. As you solve each clue and uncover the answers, hidden stories and secrets of the locations will be revealed. You have the freedom to start, pause, and explore at your own pace, ensuring a personalized adventure. The tour typically takes 1.5 to 2 hours to complete, but there's no time limit, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in this captivating experience.

Your Itinerary

Start

To start the game, you have to be at the Westminster metro station.

National Gallery

Explore the National Gallery, one of the filming locations for the Harry Potter movies. Marvel at the impressive collection of artwork and discover the secrets hidden within the paintings.

Trafalgar Square

Visit Trafalgar Square, another iconic location featured in the Harry Potter movies. Learn about the history of the square and its significance in the wizarding world.

Explore Cecil Court, the inspiration for Diagon Alley in the Harry Potter books. Immerse yourself in the magical atmosphere of this hidden gem and discover the unique shops and bookstores.

Wander through Covent Garden, a vibrant area where scenes from the Harry Potter movies were filmed. Experience the bustling market, street performers, and charming shops and cafes.
